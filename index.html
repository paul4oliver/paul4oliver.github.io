Hello, thank you for stopping by! My name is Paul Oliver and I created this website to share a little information about myself and to showcase some of my projects from school. I am a student at Southern New Hampshire University and studying for a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with a concentration in Software Engineering. This journey was a long time coming and I have greatly enjoyed making the most of it.

I like to take courses through Udacity in addition to my studies at SNHU. Last year I took AWS Cloud Foundations and Machine Learning. At the beginning of the year I started taking advantage of the GitHub Student Developer Pack. I created this website using Bootstrap Studio and am eager to explore more of the offers  that this opportunity provides. Thanks GitHub!

I also really like plants. Gardening is one of my newest hobbies and it helps create a balance in my life so that I am not always in front of a computer screen. Please enjoy pictures of my garden and some of the plants I have grown while you are here. This website is a work in  progress and I plan to update it regularly as I learn more. 

Projects

CS-300 DSA: Analysis and Design allowed me to study the implementation of various data structures in this project. Data structures have different  runtime/space complexities for different functions and this is important to consider when selecting the right one for the situation.
